About me

Hello, I am Roman Cernenko, and my passion is all about running. Here I will share with you news from my running space. But Let me first start with little history how I became a runner.
Well it was 2014 when I arrived to UK, that time I didn't run, but I had a dream to run marathon. I don't know how, but just suddenly I found some advertising about Bristol Half-Marathon, and I signed-up straigt away. There I became addicted, I started to use running trackers, I bought running watch I actually started invest all my income to running. Once I was super confident with this little distance I decided to try Marathon, Ultra-marathon and so on...
